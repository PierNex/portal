Payment Timing & Processing Cutoff: Wednesday 12:00 PM CST  ·  ACH: Thursday  ·  Delivery: Friday
📅 Standard Payment Schedule ACH
📥
All invoices must be submitted to the AP inbox or Procore by Wednesday 12:00 PM CST of the week payment is needed.
🏦
Payments are initiated via ACH on Thursday afternoons, with funds typically delivered to vendor accounts by Friday afternoon.
📌
Note: Invoice dates for invoices facilitated through Procore are based on the date submitted to Procore.
⚠️
Early submission required for invoices due Mon–Wed: If a due date falls on a Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday, the invoice must be submitted the prior Wednesday by 12:00 PM CST — one full week in advance — to be included in that Friday's payment run.

Invoices received after the Wednesday 12:00 PM CST cutoff will be processed the following week.
💸 Wire Transfers Immediate
📝
For payments governed by a signed agreement requiring immediate disbursement (e.g., LNTP, deposit), please bypass the standard AP process and route directly through Treasury.
